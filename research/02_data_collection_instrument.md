# Data Collection Instrument — CareConnect Research Paper

## Instrument Type: Structured Questionnaire (Google Forms)

**Distribution**: Online (Google Forms link shared via WhatsApp and email)
**Scale**: 5-point Likert Scale (1 = Strongly Disagree, 5 = Strongly Agree)
**Duration**: ~8 minutes per participant

---

## Section A: Participant Demographics (3 Questions)

| Q# | Question | Type |
|----|----------|------|
| A1 | What is your age group? (18–25 / 26–40 / 41–60 / 60+) | MCQ |
| A2 | What is your occupation? (Student / Professional / Patient / Other) | MCQ |
| A3 | How often do you use health/medicine apps? (Daily / Weekly / Rarely / Never) | MCQ |

---

## Section B: System Usability (5 Questions — Likert 1–5)

| Q# | Question |
|----|----------|
| B1 | The app interface was easy to understand and navigate. |
| B2 | I was able to find the features I needed without difficulty. |
| B3 | The AI assistant (Maya) responded clearly to my health questions. |
| B4 | The prescription scanning feature accurately extracted medicine details. |
| B5 | I felt confident using this app to manage my health information. |

---

## Section C: AI & Feature Quality (4 Questions — Likert 1–5)

| Q# | Question |
|----|----------|
| C1 | Maya's responses were relevant and medically appropriate. |
| C2 | The prescription scan helped me understand my medicines better. |
| C3 | The medicine reminder system would improve my adherence to prescriptions. |
| C4 | The medical report analysis was easy to understand for a non-medical person. |

---

## Section D: Overall Satisfaction (2 Questions)

| Q# | Question | Type |
|----|----------|------|
| D1 | Overall, how satisfied are you with CareConnect? (1–5 stars) | Rating |
| D2 | Would you recommend CareConnect to family or friends? (Yes / No / Maybe) | MCQ |

---

## Section E: Open-Ended Feedback (2 Questions)

| Q# | Question |
|----|----------|
| E1 | What feature did you find most useful? (short answer) |
| E2 | What improvement would you suggest? (short answer) |

---

## Scoring Key

| Score Range | Interpretation |
|-------------|----------------|
| 4.5 – 5.0 | Excellent |
| 3.5 – 4.4 | Good |
| 2.5 – 3.4 | Average |
| Below 2.5 | Needs Improvement |
