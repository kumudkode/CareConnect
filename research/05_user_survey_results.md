## Key Findings from Survey

- **Highest rated**: Medicine reminder feature (4.47) and prescription scanning helpfulness (4.43)
- **Lowest rated**: Medical report analysis clarity (4.07) — patients found complex lab terms harder to understand
- **Elderly patients (46+)**: slightly lower scores on navigation (4.1) — suggests need for larger fonts and simplified UI
- **Students**: most enthusiastic users with consistently highest ratings
